The Marcell Masonic Hall is pleased to consider rental inquiries from organizations, businesses and individuals from the Lakewood/West Shore community for use of our Ballroom and Banquet Room. Our facilities are especially suitable for fund raising events, dances, weddings, receptions, parties, banquets, performances, meetings, seminars, trade shows and the like and will accommodate groups up to 450 persons in a single hall.
