Membership

Qualifications to become a Prince Hall Freemason in Ohio you must:

  • Be a man and at least 18 years of age.
  • Be a citizen of Ohio
  • Be of good moral character
  • Have a personal belief in a Supreme Being.
  • Decide to become a Mason of "your own free will and accord.
  • Be loyal to your country.
  • Dedicated to providing for your own family.
  • Have a sincere determination to conduct yourself in a manner that will earn the respet and trust of others.
  • Possess a desire to help others through community service and universal benevolence.
  • Be of good mental and physical health, and have good control and use of your limbs.

Follow these steps to become a member

  1. Talk with someone you know who is a member of the Fraternity.
  2. If you do not know anyone who is a Mason, just contact a lodge in your neighborhood.
  3. Masons do not solicit for members. You'll need to express your personal interest in joining the Fraternity.
  4. Complete a petition for membership, obtain three signatures of members of the Lodge you are petitioning and return it to your local Lodge with your petition fees.
  5. Three members will meet with you to answer your questions.
  6. Your petition will be presented for secret ballot. If accepted, you will be notified to appear to receive the first of three Degrees of Masonry.
